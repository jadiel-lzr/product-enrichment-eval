# Roadmap: Product Enrichment Evaluation

## Overview

Side-by-side comparison of 4 enrichment tools (Claude, Gemini, FireCrawl, Perplexity) across ~500 real products, presented in a React UI where the client can filter, score, and analyze results to choose the best enrichment strategy.
